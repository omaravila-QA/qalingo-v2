QALingo is an app made by a QA professional who got tired of preparing for interviews with boring PDFs and long videos.

I decided to create a practical, fun, and effective method — a Duolingo for QA where you can review fundamentals, prep for ISTQB, and nail interview questions in just 5 minutes a day.

With the help of Q — that blue bug detective with the soul of James Bond and the mind of Sherlock Holmes — learning no longer feels like studying... it feels like solving interesting cases.
